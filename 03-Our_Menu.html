    THE MENU

    Our menu has been crafted with equal importance to North and South Indian food – dishes that are wholesome, delicious and make for a perfect meal with family and friends. Matched with a choice of beverages and desserts, our menu aims to pack a solid punch.

    From the classic Chola Bhatura to the almost-exotic Karam Podi Dosa, Uttar Dakshin has the best of both worlds. A mind-boggling variety of dosas and the perfect offset of North Indian dishes, our menu is a balance of both these exciting worlds.
